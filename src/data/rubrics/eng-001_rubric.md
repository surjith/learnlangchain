# English Assignment Rubric – Persuasive Writing

## Task Overview
Students are required to write a persuasive text that presents a clear viewpoint on a given topic and supports it with logical arguments and evidence.

---

## Assessment Criteria

### 1. Ideas and Argument (10 marks)
- Presents a clear and consistent position
- Uses relevant reasons and examples to support arguments
- Addresses opposing viewpoints where appropriate

**High (8–10):**
Clear, compelling argument with well-developed ideas and strong supporting evidence.

**Medium (5–7):**
Argument is mostly clear with some supporting examples.

**Low (0–4):**
Ideas are unclear or lack supporting evidence.

---

### 2. Structure and Organisation (8 marks)
- Effective introduction and conclusion
- Logical paragraphing
- Clear progression of ideas

**High (7–8):**
Text is well structured and easy to follow.

**Medium (4–6):**
Some organisation is evident but may be inconsistent.

**Low (0–3):**
Poorly organised with limited structure.

---

### 3. Language Features (7 marks)
- Persuasive language devices
- Vocabulary choice
- Sentence variety

**High (6–7):**
Uses persuasive language effectively and accurately.

**Medium (3–5):**
Some persuasive language used with occasional errors.

**Low (0–2):**
Limited or ineffective use of language features.

---

### Total: 25 marks
