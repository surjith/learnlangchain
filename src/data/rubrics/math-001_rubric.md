# Mathematics Assignment Rubric – Algebra Test

## Task Overview
Students will complete an algebra test assessing their understanding of linear equations and basic algebraic manipulation.

---

## Assessment Criteria

### 1. Understanding of Concepts (10 marks)
- Correct use of algebraic rules
- Accurate solving of equations
- Demonstrates understanding of variables and constants

**High (8–10):**
Shows strong understanding and applies concepts accurately.

**Medium (5–7):**
Shows general understanding with minor errors.

**Low (0–4):**
Limited understanding of algebraic concepts.

---

### 2. Working and Reasoning (10 marks)
- Shows clear working
- Logical steps used in problem solving
- Answers are justified

**High (8–10):**
All working is shown clearly and logically.

**Medium (5–7):**
Most working is shown but may lack clarity.

**Low (0–4):**
Little or no working shown.

---

### 3. Accuracy (5 marks)
- Correct final answers
- Minimal calculation errors

**High (5):**
All answers are correct.

**Medium (3–4):**
Most answers are correct.

**Low (0–2):**
Many incorrect answers.

---

### Total: 25 marks
