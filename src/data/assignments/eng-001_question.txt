English Assignment – Persuasive Writing Task

Topic:
“Should homework be reduced for high school students?”

Task Description:
Write a persuasive text arguing either for or against reducing homework for high school students.

You should:
- Clearly state your opinion in the introduction
- Provide at least three reasons to support your viewpoint
- Include examples to strengthen your arguments
- Consider and respond to at least one opposing viewpoint
- Conclude by reinforcing your main position

Length:
400–500 words

Audience:
Your writing should be suitable for a general audience, including teachers and parents.

Submission:
Submit your completed task by the due date provided by your teacher.
